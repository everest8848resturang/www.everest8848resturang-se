About Everest 8848

Everest 8848 brings the authentic flavors of Nepal to Sweden. Our chefs prepare each dish using traditional recipes, fresh ingredients, and a touch of Himalayan hospitality.

What We Offer

  • Authentic Nepalese dishes (momos, thukpa, curries)
  • Breakfast, beverages, and refreshing drinks
  • Venue hire for small events, family gatherings, and office lunches
  • A warm, welcoming atmosphere for friends and family

Our goal is to share the rich culinary heritage of Nepal while providing an unforgettable dining experience in Stockholm, Sweden.
